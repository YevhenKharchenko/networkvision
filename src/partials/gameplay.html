HOW THE SNAILS MOVE

Every level asks you to think before you slide.

  • The controls are intentionally simple:

    • Drag from a snail’s body

    • Slide it across the board

    • Release and let it glide

  • But the challenge comes from everything around it.

    • Snails can block each other

    • Paths overlap and cross

    • One wrong move can trap another snail
