How to Play

Looks simple at first, but man, once you're juggling customers and upgrades and everything... it gets complicated fast.

  • 1

    Inspect incoming devices to identify issues

  • 2

    Perform repairs like screen replacement, battery swaps, cleaning and diagnostics

  • 3

    Earn currency for successful repairs

  • 4

    Upgrade tools, unlock new parts and expand the shop

  • 5

    Serve customers efficiently to grow reputation and revenue

Features & Benefits

You're basically the boss of everything - which tools to buy, keeping customers happy, all that stuff.

  • The repair stuff is surprisingly detailed - like, you're actually doing the work instead of just clicking random buttons.

  • Upgrade path for tooling and workshop space

  • Great for quick breaks or when you just want to chill for a while

  • There's always something new to work toward, which keeps me coming back

Our Uniqueness
